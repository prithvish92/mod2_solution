Pokemon Go Teams

Valor

Candela is in charge of the Team Valor (Red). The team's mascot is Moltres. Team Valor has earned a reputation online for being the most competitive and fiercest of the three teams.

Mystic

Blanche is the head of Team Mystic (blue). The icy legendary Articuno is Team Mystic's mascot. The team is guided by intellect, wisdom and calmness. It is the most popular of the three teams.

Instinct

Spark is the leader of the yellow team (Team Instinct). Zapdos, the legendary bird, is this team's mascot. The team is guided by gut-feelings and trust. It appears to be the least popular choice.
